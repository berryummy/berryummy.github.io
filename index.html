Mental Health & Substance Abuse in Teens

Our mission is to spread awareness about substance abuse among youth and inspire other teens to encourage themselves and friends to not do drugs! These illistrations were created by Anna Laura on Instagram! Click here to view Anna's art account.

Understanding and accepting what you are feeling can be difficult, that is why its improtant to remember it's okay to not be okay.

When a teen feels lonely it can make them run to drugs to numb their emotions but an effective coping strategy such as journaling has shown to work because it allows the person the express their thoughts and feeling instead of pushing them aside.

Surrounding yourself with people who care for your recovery can speed the process and give you the stenght to keep your mental health better.
